About

This site goes through the information for a new structured clan called The Token Klan. The Token Klan will be a clan of members that respect each other. The clan will have a friendly and inviting atmosphere. This atmosphere will be achieved through friendly battles critiquing the way members battle to help them win more. But this clan won't be so uptight about the rules and norms, this clan will be relaxed but not taken to far. Basically in this clan is a group of civilized people.

Norms of this clan

The First norm is, Never leave a 2v2 battle with a clan member. The Second norm is, Absolutely no cursing under any circumstance. The Third norm is, Always request a war battle to the clan to test your war deck if no one responses make that known in the chat. The Fourth norm is, Always donate one card will do. The Fifth norm is, Always always try your best. The Sixth norm is, NEVER quit.

The clan name

The clan name can be changed through a vote in the clan chat.
